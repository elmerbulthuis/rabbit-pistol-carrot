Story example

As a person in disagreement I want to play the game with the other person so that we have a fair way of settling our disagreement.

Story detail example

Each player picks a symbol. The player that picks the symbol that trumps the other wins. Rock trumps scissors, scissors trumps paper, paper trumps rock. If they both pick the same then it's a tie and they play again until there is a winner.
